Founder Handoff Coaching

Coach the founder out of backend dependency with clearer ownership, stronger handoffs, and less operational memory load.

What this solves

When the founder is still the answer key for every backend question, the business cannot hand off cleanly. This coaching helps shift that dependence into documented ownership.

  • Team members still ask the founder what happens next
  • Processes exist, but nobody owns them clearly
  • Handoffs break because knowledge is still verbal

What you get

  • Clearer role ownership across the backend
  • Stronger handoff language and expectations
  • Less founder dependence in day-to-day execution

Best fit

This is best for founders who are growing a team, but still feel pulled back into the backend because the business has not fully handed off the operating layer yet.
