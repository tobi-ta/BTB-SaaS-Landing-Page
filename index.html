FAQ

Answering hesitation makes the site feel more complete.

Is this for businesses under $1M?

Usually not the core fit. BTB is strongest when the business has already grown enough that the backend is creating drag.

Do you build custom software?

No. BTB configures and connects proven tools around the operation instead of selling custom dev as the default answer.

Do we need to change our tools?

Not always. BTB is tool-agnostic, so the right answer depends on what already exists and what the operation truly needs.

What happens after the audit?

You get a clear picture of the backend, a roadmap, and a better sense of whether full ownership is actually needed.

Do you only work in one industry?

No. The edge comes partly from seeing patterns across different service businesses and applying what actually works.

What kind of founder is the best fit?

A founder who wants the backend handled well, not someone looking for DIY advice or one-off software tasks.
