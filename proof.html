Featured Case Study

A 12-person agency that stopped running everything through the founder.

Creative agency · 90-day engagement

The situation

Every client decision, pipeline update, and handoff still passed through the founder. Team members constantly asked "what's next?" because nothing was documented and the CRM was a wall of half-filled fields.

What we changed

Restructured the CRM around real operational stages, automated the repeating workflows between sales and delivery, and coached two senior team members to take full ownership of backend decisions.

The result

The founder now spends 6 hours/week on ops instead of 25. The team launched three campaigns back-to-back without a single escalation. Onboarding new hires dropped from 4 weeks to 10 days.
