About Behind The Business

WE HELP FOUNDERS STOP BEING THE BACKEND.

Behind The Business is built for founder-led companies that have outgrown memory-based operations but still run on founder knowledge, fragmented tools, and invisible systems.

Our Mission

Turn founder memory into real systems.

Most growing businesses don't have a revenue problem - they have a backend problem. The founder becomes the single point of failure, operations depend on memory, and scale adds drag instead of clarity.

We rebuild the backend so that what's inside the founder's head becomes a system the team can actually run. CRM structure, workflow automation, handoff coaching, and operational ownership - so the founder can scale without being the bottleneck.

Meet the Founder

I started Behind The Business after watching three founder friends burn out.

Before starting BTB, I was working closely with early-stage startups, helping them navigate growth and operations. Over time, I noticed a troubling pattern: many founders were pushing themselves to the brink, sacrificing their health and relationships. The turning point came when three close friends, all founders, experienced severe burnout within months of each other. That was the moment I realized something had to change, and I felt compelled to build a solution.

In founder-led businesses, I kept seeing the same issues repeat. Founders were wearing too many hats, struggling with decision fatigue, and constantly firefighting instead of building sustainably. They often lacked systems to delegate effectively, leaving them overwhelmed and isolated. Despite these being common struggles, no one seemed to be addressing them in a structured, proactive way. The frustration was clear: brilliant people were burning out before their ideas had a chance to thrive.

I want founders to build businesses without sacrificing themselves in the process. My goal is to help them create companies that are both successful and sustainable, where they can thrive personally as well as professionally. At the heart of it, I want to see founders enjoy the journey, not just survive it.

Tobey Tobias Founder — Behind The Business
